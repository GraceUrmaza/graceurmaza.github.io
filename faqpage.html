Comissions

What are comissions?

Comissions are custom artworks that I make specifically for you! You request the subject and the size of the art piece and we can discuss from there :)

How can I commission you?

Please go to the "Support Me" page and read the instructions under the "Comissions" section.

What can I commission from you?

I love drawing portraits of pets and people. I have also designed tattoos for myself and others, but I am mainly used to working in digital media and acrylic paints. I am very interested in exprminenting with mediums!

How much is a comission?

That depends on the size, medium, and subject of the comission. Once I know the specifics of your order, I will give you a rough estimate of price.

How does payment work?

Once the price is agreed upon, I will take half of the payment upfront and half of the payment after I am finished with the comission. The payment process will be done through PayPal or Etsy.

How long does it take for you to make a comission?

This also depends on size, medium, and subject of the comission.

Will you give me updates on my comission?

I will send you a picture of my sketches, intermediate phase, and final rendering of your comission.

My Art Process

How did you develop your art style?

Finding an art style is a continuous process. I learned the foundations of art first and messed around with porportions, colors, and differnt mediums and kept what I liked.

What is your favorite art supply?

I could not live without my iPad and Apple Pencil. They are my best friends for creating digital art. I am also a huge fan of travel watercolor kits even though watercolor is not my favorite medium.

Did you go to school for art?

I only took two art classes in high school. I am mostly self taught through YouTube videos and practice.

How did you get started with art?

I loved arts and crafts since I was a young child. I began with crochet and then moved onto clay.

What advice do you have for beginner artists?

Learn the foundations of art first and draw with references!!!! Your art style will come later on. I recommend Proko, and Sinix Design on YouTube.
